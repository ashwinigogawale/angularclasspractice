Ashtavinayak Ganpati

"Ashtavinayak" or "Eight Ganpati" is located in Maharashtra state of India, The Asthtavinayak yatra or pilgrimage covers the eight holy temples of Ganpati. All the Eight Ashtavinayak temples are Swayambhu (self-originated) and Jagrut. the various names of Lord Ganesh are Moreshwar, Mahaganpati, Chintamani, Girijatmak, Vighneshwar, Siddhivinayak, Ballaleshwar and Varad Vinayak.

  • Day 01

    Departure from Pune to Morgaon (Mayureshwar) the first Ganpati in Ashtavinayak. After darshan proceed to Siddhatek (Siddhivinayak). After darshan lunch at Daund and proceed to Theur (Chintamani). After darshan proceed to Ranjangaon (Mahaganpati). Later proceed to Ozar (Vighneshwar). After darshan, dinner and overnight stay at Ozar.

  • Day 02

    Early morning proceed to Lenyadri (Girijatmaj), it is situated on a hill. After darshan, breakfast and proceed to Mahad (Varadvinayak). After darshan Lunch and proceed to Pali (Ballaleshwar). After darshan proceed to Pune.
